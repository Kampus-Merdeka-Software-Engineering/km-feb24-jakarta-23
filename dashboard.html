Daily Trends for Total Orders

Insight

The line graph displaying the total number of orders each day over a week suggests a noticeable spike in sales on Fridays, indicating a potential opportunity to maximize revenue on this particular day.


Recommendation

To maximize sales, focus on boosting promotions and staffing during peak days, particularly Fridays, by offering special deals, ensuring sufficient inventory, enhancing operational efficiency, and establishing partnerships to accommodate the high activity levels.

Sales Performance by Size

Insight

The donut chart representing sales distribution based on pizza size highlights a preference for extra-large (XL) and double-extra-large (XXL) pizzas.
